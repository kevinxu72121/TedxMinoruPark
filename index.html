BEGINNING

What is Minoru Park?

Welcome to Minoru Park, a serene haven nestled in the heart of Richmond, BC. With its lush greenery, peaceful ponds, and winding paths, Minoru Park offers a picturesque retreat for visitors seeking tranquility. Whether you're looking to enjoy a leisurely walk amidst nature, have a family picnic on the expansive lawns, or engage in recreational activities like tennis or lawn bowling, Minoru Park has something for everyone. Immerse yourself in the beauty of this hidden gem and experience the serenity and relaxation that Minoru Park has to offer.

How It Started

TEDxMinoru Park Youth is a new event licensed in May 2023, marking the first TEDx event in Richmond, BC in over two years. We are excited to bring TEDx back to our community and aim to make it a consecutive annual event. Our team is committed to creating an unforgettable experience that inspires and empowers the youth of Richmond. With the support of TED, we are determined to foster a platform for ideas worth spreading and contribute to the intellectual growth of our local community.

What's Next

We are excited to host TEDxMinoru Park Youth as an annual event. Each year, we will curate a lineup of inspiring speakers, engaging workshops, and thought-provoking performances to create an unforgettable experience that sparks creativity and drives positive change.
